Our Story

A cozy corner where time slows down and flavor comes alive

More Than Just a Cafe

Old Soul Vintage Cafe was born from a love for slow mornings, rich conversations, and the simple joy of good food. Nestled near Raisoni College of Engineering and Management, we've become a second home for students, professionals, and families alike.

Our vintage-inspired interiors, handcrafted beverages, and soulful menu are designed to make every visit memorable. From our signature pour-over coffees to our freshly baked pastries, every detail is crafted with love.

Whether you're studying for exams, catching up with friends, or just seeking a quiet moment — Old Soul welcomes you with open arms.
