The Art of Wood Story

Located on Grant Road, Art of Wood has been a trusted destination for premium handcrafted furniture for over two decades. Our journey began with a simple belief: that truly great furniture is born from the marriage of exceptional materials and master craftsmanship.

We source only the finest solid woods — teak, rosewood, sheesham, and oak — ensuring every piece not only looks stunning but stands the test of time. Our artisans carry forward age-old woodworking traditions, blending them seamlessly with contemporary design sensibilities.
