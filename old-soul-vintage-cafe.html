About Us

A timeless experience since day one

Our Story

Old Soul Vintage Cafe was born from a passion for bringing people together over great food and even greater conversations. Nestled in Wagholi, Pune, our cafe blends vintage aesthetics with modern culinary artistry.

Every corner of our space is designed to make you feel at home — from the warm lighting and rustic wooden furniture to the aroma of freshly brewed coffee that greets you at the door.
