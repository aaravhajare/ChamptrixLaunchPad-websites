About Us

A Café Steeped in Tradition

Old Soul Vintage Cafe was born from a love of slow mornings, rich aromas, and the warmth of community. Nestled in the heart of Wagholi, Pune, we blend vintage aesthetics with modern flavors.

Every dish on our menu is crafted with passion, every corner of our café tells a story, and every cup served carries a piece of our heritage. Whether you're a student from Raisoni College or a traveler passing through, you'll always find a seat and a smile here.
