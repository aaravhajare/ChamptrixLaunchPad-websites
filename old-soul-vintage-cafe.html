Our Story

A place where tradition meets modern taste

Brewed with Love, Served with Soul

Old Soul Vintage Cafe was born from a simple belief — that great coffee and good company can transform any day. Nestled near Raisoni College of Engineering and Management in Wagholi, Pune, we've become a beloved neighborhood gem.

Our vintage-inspired interiors are designed to make you feel right at home, with warm tones, rustic charm, and an atmosphere that invites you to slow down and savor the moment.
