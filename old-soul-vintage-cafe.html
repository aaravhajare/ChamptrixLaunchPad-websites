Our Story

A place where vintage charm meets modern comfort

Welcome to Old Soul Vintage Cafe

Nestled in the heart of Wagholi, Pune, Old Soul Vintage Cafe is more than just a coffee spot — it's a retreat from the chaos of everyday life. Our vintage-inspired interiors and warm ambiance create the perfect setting for meaningful conversations and quiet moments.

We source the finest beans, prepare our recipes with love, and serve every customer like family. Whether you're here for your morning brew or an evening treat, we promise an experience that lingers long after you leave.
