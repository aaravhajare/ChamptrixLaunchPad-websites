Our Story

A Culinary Journey

Nestled in the heart of Wagholi, Pune, Greystone Restaurant & Banquet is your destination for an unforgettable dining experience. We bring together the finest North Indian, Chinese, Asian, and Continental cuisines under one roof.

From aromatic biryanis to mouth-watering kebabs, from exotic Asian flavors to delectable desserts – every dish is crafted with passion, precision, and the freshest ingredients.

Whether it's an intimate dinner, a grand celebration, or a casual family meal, we create moments that linger in your memory long after the last bite.
