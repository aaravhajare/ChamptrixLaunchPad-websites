FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our design process and services.

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A single room redesign may take 4-6 weeks, while a full home renovation can take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your design fee structure?

+

We offer flexible fee structures including flat project fees, hourly rates, and percentage-based pricing. During our consultation, we'll discuss the best approach for your specific project and budget.

Do you work with existing furniture?

+

Absolutely! We love incorporating pieces that have sentimental value or that you already love. Our designers are skilled at blending existing pieces with new selections to create a cohesive look.

What areas do you serve?

+

We primarily serve the metropolitan area and surrounding regions. For destination projects, we're happy to discuss travel arrangements. Contact us to see if we service your area.

Can I be involved in the design process?

+

We encourage client collaboration! Your input is invaluable in creating a space that truly reflects your personality. We'll guide you through each decision while ensuring the final result is cohesive and beautiful.
