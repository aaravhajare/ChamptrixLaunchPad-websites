Our Story

Crafted with Passion, Served with Love

Greystone Restaurant & Banquet is more than a dining destination — it's an experience. Nestled in the heart of Wagholi, Pune, we bring together the rich heritage of Indian cuisine with the bold flavors of Asian and Continental cooking.

Every dish is a masterpiece, prepared with the freshest ingredients, authentic spices, and a commitment to making every guest feel like family. Whether it's a grand banquet or an intimate dinner, we make every occasion unforgettable.
