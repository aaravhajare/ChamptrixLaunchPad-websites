About Us

A place crafted with love, passion, and the finest ingredients

Our Story

Old Soul Vintage Cafe was born from a simple dream — to create a space where people can slow down, savor life, and connect over great food and coffee. Nestled near Raisoni College in Wagholi, our cafe has become a beloved gathering spot for students, professionals, and families alike.

We source our ingredients locally, brew our coffee with care, and design every dish to delight your senses. From our signature espresso to our handcrafted pastries, every item on our menu is a testament to quality and passion.
