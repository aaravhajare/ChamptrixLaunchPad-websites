Where Champions Are Made

PowerFit Gym is Pune's premier fitness destination, offering world-class training facilities designed to help you achieve your fitness goals. Our 10,000 sq ft facility is equipped with the latest strength and cardio equipment.

Founded in 2016, we've built a community of passionate fitness enthusiasts who support and motivate each other every single day.
